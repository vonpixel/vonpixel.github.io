Lo City
---
Lo City was created for the shawarma mech to stomp around in and provide a set for a cinematic. This project was created a year into the pandemic when I started to miss night time in the city. I wanted a futuristic aesthetic but I also wanted it to feel warm and happy.
---
Modo, Unreal, Substance Painter, Reality Capture, Polycam, Photoshop, Quixel
